About Seedlink


Seedlink is a site for ordering seeds and sharing produce. You can either grow a beautiful tree and help our planet or grow some fruit or vegetables. Once your friuts and/or vegetables are ready you can share your excess produce with your local community.

Just log how much extra produce you have on the donation page and we will collect or you can bring them to our weekley market. These will be shared with anyone that needs them for free.

You dont need to get the seeds from us in order to share. Any fruit or vegetables can be gifted to the market.

so join in, get outside and become a planter. You can promote plan based eating, help the environment and your local community along the way.
