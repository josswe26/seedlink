Marketplace

Every Saturday in the main Yard, 10am-4pm

On Saturdays our marketplace brings together lots of fresh excess produce to share with the community for free!

Stalls vary from week to week, offering a range of vegetables, fruit and seeds. You can see what's available live below!

We are passionate in eliminating food waste by sharing surplus produce within the locally community. Nothing ever gets wasted! Any leftover produce will get delivered to local foodbanks at the end of the day.
